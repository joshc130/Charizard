Charizard

Charizard is a dual-type Fire/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Charmeleon starting at level 36. It is the final form of Charmander.

Characteristics

  • Type: Fire/Flying
  • Height: 1.7 m (5'07")
  • Weight: 90.5 kg (199.5 lbs)
  • Abilities: Blaze, Solar Power (Hidden Ability)

Moves

  • Flamethrower
  • Fly
  • Dragon Claw
  • Fire Spin
